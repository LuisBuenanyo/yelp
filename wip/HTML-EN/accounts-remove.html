Remove an account

  1. Open the Online Accounts settings from the Activities overview.

  2. Select the account which you wish to remove.

  3. Click the - button in the lower-left portion of the window.

  4. Click Remove.

Instead of deleting the account completely, it's possible to restrict the services accessed by your desktop.

To remove GNOME's access to the online account, you should revoke the access through the account's website or similar service.
