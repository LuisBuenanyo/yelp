Change the date, time and timezone

  1. Click the clock on the top bar and select the Date & Time Settings item.

  2. Make sure that the Automatic Time Zone item is switched off, and then click the Time Zone item below.

  3. Click on your location on the map. This selects your current city, which you can also search for in the search box above the map.

  4. Click the Close button to go back to the Date & Time Settings window.

  5. Make sure that the Automatic Date & Time item is switched off, and then click the Date & Time item below to open the Date & Time window. There you can adjust your date and time settings by clicking on the + or - buttons.

  6. Click the Close button to go back to the Date & Time Settings window, and then close that window by clicking the cross at the top-right corner of that window.
