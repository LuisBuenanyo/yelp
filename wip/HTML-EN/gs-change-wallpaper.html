Change the wallpaper

  1. Click the system menu on the right side of the top bar and press the settings button at the bottom of the menu.

  2. From the list of items, select Background.

  3. Click the current background image on the left side of the Background window.

  4. Click the background image you want to use.

  5. Click the Select button.

  6. Close the Background window by clicking the cross at the top-right corner of the window.
