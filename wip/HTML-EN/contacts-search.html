Search for a contact

You can search for an online contact in one of two ways:

  1. In the Activities overview, start typing the name of the contact.

  2. Matching contacts will appear in the overview instead of the usual list of applications.

  3. Press Enter to select the contact at the top of the list or click the contact that you want to select if they are not at the top.

To search from inside Contacts:

  1. Click inside the search field.

  2. Start typing the name of the contact.
