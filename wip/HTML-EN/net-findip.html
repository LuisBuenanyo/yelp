Find your IP address

Knowing your IP address can help you troubleshoot problems with your internet connection. You may be surprised to learn that you have two IP addresses: an IP address for your computer on the internal network and an IP address for your computer on the internet.

Find your internal (network) IP address

  1. Open the Activities overview and start typing Network.

  2. Click on Network to open the panel.

  3. Select Wi-Fi or Wired from the list on the left, depending on which network connection you want to find the IP address for.

  4. You will see the IP address for a wired connection on the right, or open the connection details by pressing on the button to see the Details for wireless network.

Find your external (internet) IP address

  1. Visit whatismyipaddress.com.

  2. The site will display your external IP address for you.

Depending on how your computer connects to the internet, both of these addresses may be the same.
