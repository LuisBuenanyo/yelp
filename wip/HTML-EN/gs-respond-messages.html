Respond to a chat message with the mouse

  1. Move your mouse to the message tray at the bottom of the screen and click the chat message.

  2. Start typing your reply and when finished, press Enter to send the reply.

  3. To close the chat message, click the close button at the top right corner of the chat message.

Delayed response to a chat message using the mouse

  1. When a chat message appears in the message tray and you do not move your mouse to the message tray, the message disappears after a while.

  2. To get back to your unanswered message, move your mouse to the message tray at the very bottom of the screen.

  3. When the message tray appears, click a small image that represents the person who sent you the message.

  4. When the chat message is shown, start typing your reply and when finished, press Enter to send the reply.

  5. To close the chat message, click the close button at the top right corner of the chat message.

Delayed response to a chat message using the keyboard

  1. To get back to your unanswered chat messages, press Super+M to display the message tray that contains the messages.

  2. Use the arrow keys to select a small image representing the person you want to reply to, and press Enter.

  3. Start typing your reply and when finished, press Enter to send the reply.

  4. Press Esc to close the chat message.

  5. To dismiss the message tray, press Esc or Super+M.
