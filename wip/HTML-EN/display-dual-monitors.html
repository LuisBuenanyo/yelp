Set up an additional monitor

To set up an additional monitor, connect the monitor to your computer. If your system does not recognize it immediately, or you would like to adjust the settings:

  1. Open the Activities overview and start typing Displays.

  2. Click on Displays to open the panel.

  3. The monitor with the top bar is the main monitor. To change which monitor is treated as "main", click on the top bar and drag it over to the monitor which you want to set as the "main" monitor.

  4. To rotate the orientation of the monitor, click on it and use the buttons with the arrows to rotate it.

  5. When you are happy with your settings, click Apply and then click Keep Changes.
