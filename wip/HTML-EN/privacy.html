Privacy Settings

The Privacy Settings in GNOME help you control whether or not certain parts of your desktop are visible to others. You can also use these settings to clear your computer usage history and clean-out unecessary files.

Automatically lock your screen — Prevent other people from using your desktop when you go away from your computer.
Purge trash & temporary files — Set how often your trash and temporary files will be cleared from your computer.
Turn off file history tracking — Stop your computer from tracking your recently-used files.
