Change date and measurement formats

You can control the formats that are used for dates, times, numbers, currency, and measurement to match the local customs of your region.

  1. Open the Activities overview and start typing Region & Language.

  2. Click on Region & Language to open the panel.

  3. Click Formats.

  4. Select the region that most closely matches the formats you'd like to use. By default, the list only shows regions that use the language set on the Language tab. Click the ... button to select from all available regions.

  5. Respond to the prompt, Your session needs to be restarted for these changes to take effect by clicking Restart Now, or click X to restart later.

After you've selected a region, the area to the right of the list shows various examples of how dates and other values are shown. Although not shown in the examples, your region also controls the starting day of the week in calendars.
