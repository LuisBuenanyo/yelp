Change your timezone

  1. Click on the clock in the middle of the top bar and select Date & Time Settings.

  2. If you have a standard account, you will need to click Unlock and type the administrator password.

  3. If you have Automatic Time Zone set to On, your time zone should update automatically if you have an internet connection. To update your time zone manually, set this to Off.

  4. Click Time Zone, then select your location on the map or search for your current city.

The time will be updated automatically when you select a different location. You may also wish to set the clock manually.
