Change the date and time

If the date and time displayed on the top bar are incorrect or in the wrong format, you can change them:

  1. Click on the clock in the middle of the top bar and select Date & Time Settings.

  2. If you have a standard account, you will need to click Unlock and type the administrator password.

  3. If you have an internet connection, you can set your date, time and time zone to update automatically by switching Automatic Date & Time and Automatic Time Zone to On.

    If you do not have an internet connection, you can update these manually by switching them to Off, then clicking Date & Time and Time Zone to update.

  4. You can also change how the hour is displayed by selecting 24-hour or AM/PM format.
